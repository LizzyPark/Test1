ALRA FOREST란 무엇인가?

Alra Forest is started by ALRAPARK who is founder. This website provides users with an interesting explorience. As your choices, learn how different plant and animal species form ecosystem in various climates.

First of all, you can choose from cold climates to tropical climates. Once you have selected it, you will be presented with options for it.

With your choice, you can configure an ecosystem suitable for each climate in your own forest.

This experince is important not only for children but also for adults.
